# Chapter 23 - Runway Video Prompts (veo3 text-to-video)

**PROMPT 1 - Bern Federal Building Exterior**
Slow upward tilting shot of an imposing stone government building in Bern, Switzerland, with Swiss federal architecture — arched windows, sandstone facade, and a Swiss flag hanging motionless above the entrance. Early morning winter light casts long blue shadows across the cobblestone plaza. A lone figure in a dark overcoat walks toward the entrance, seen from behind in the lower third of frame. Pigeons scatter from the cobblestones. Cinematic wide-angle composition with cold blue-gray color grading emphasizing institutional authority, vertical 9:16 framing following the building's height from street level to roofline.

Overhead fluorescent lights create stark institutional lighting with geometric shadow patterns from tall windows on his left. His expression is focused and purposeful, visible only in three-quarter profile. Cool desaturated color grading with sharp contrast, vertical 9:16 composition emphasizing the long corridor and his deliberate stride.
