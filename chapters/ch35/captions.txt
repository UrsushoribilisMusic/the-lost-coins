Lena comes back from town with medical supplies
And three towers of pizza from a local joint

Paul and Chen are on equipment crates comparing cat photos
Shanghai ginger cat vs San Francisco rescue tabby

Paul raises a warm beer: "To a good day of work"
Erica echoes it. Her eyes find Lena's across the table.

There is a softness there from the Napa terrace
That the cold fluorescent lights can't quite kill

Then boots on the concrete floor
Marco steps from the shadow into the lamplight

"The transport teams want an update"
The table goes quiet. Paul's pizza stops halfway to his mouth.

Erica stands up straight: "Tell them we'll be ready tomorrow evening"
The last supper is over. The ghost is coming.
