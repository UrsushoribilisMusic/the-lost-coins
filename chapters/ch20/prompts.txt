# Chapter 20 - Runway Video Prompts (veo3 text-to-video)

**PROMPT 1 - Paul Pacing in a Small Apartment**
Profile tracking shot of a 25-year-old man, 1.80m tall, slim build, with black hair and 5-day stubble beard, wearing glasses, a faded graphic t-shirt, dark blue jeans, and colorful orange-and-blue sneakers, pacing back and forth in a small cluttered apartment. Camera follows him from the side as he gestures with his hands while talking, with a laptop open on a messy desk in the background showing spreadsheets in red. Late afternoon light streams through a small window, casting long shadows across scattered papers and energy drink cans. Cinematic shallow depth of field, slightly desaturated color grading with cool blue tones emphasizing anxiety and financial pressure, vertical 9:16 framing.
