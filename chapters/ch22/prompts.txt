# Chapter 22 - Runway Video Prompts (veo3 text-to-video)

**PROMPT 1 - The Dimly Lit Bar Near HSLU**
Wide establishing shot of a cozy, dimly lit bar interior with exposed brick walls, warm Edison bulb string lights, and dark wooden tables. Camera positioned at a low angle looking across the room where two women sit at a corner table, visible only as silhouettes and soft outlines. One has long blonde hair catching the warm light, the other has brown hair in a loose ponytail. Candle light flickers on their table creating dancing shadows on the brick wall behind them. Warm amber and honey color grading with deep shadows, cinematic shallow depth of field blurring the bar's other patrons into bokeh, vertical 9:16 framing establishing the intimate, conspiratorial atmosphere.

Warm candlelight illuminates the folder and their hands while their faces remain in atmospheric shadow. Edison bulbs create warm bokeh in the background. Cinematic shallow depth of field, warm amber tones, vertical 9:16 framing emphasizing the transfer of the fraudulent documents.
