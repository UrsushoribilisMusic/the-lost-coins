The morning after Napa is soft and blue
Lena drives them south through the Bay Area in silence

Paul dozes against the window
Erica watches the back of Lena's head and can't stop thinking

At the Caltrain station Erica says:
"Thank you for Napa." No calculation. Just truth.

Lena watches them disappear into the station
Then pulls back onto the road

Two blocks later, she sees it
Silver sedan. Three cars back. Steady. Patient.

She makes three turns. It makes three turns.
Lena does not panic. She has nowhere left to run.

The Fairmont is straight ahead
And someone is already in Room 412
