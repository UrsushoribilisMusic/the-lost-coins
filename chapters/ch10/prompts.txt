# Chapter 10 - Runway Video Prompts (veo3 text-to-video)

PROMPT 1 - Exit from the Lab
A woman in her early thirties with straight dark brown hair pulled back in a low ponytail, pale skin, athletic build, wearing a charcoal gray wool coat and black leather boots stands in three-quarter back view on a modern university plaza. The camera frames her from behind and slightly to the side as she watches another figure disappear into a distant cafeteria building. Cold wind visibly moves her coat and loose strands of hair. The lighting is overcast daylight creating a desaturated, cool color palette. Vertical 9:16 framing with shallow depth of field, the background plaza slightly blurred, emphasizing isolation and the transition from indoor to outdoor space.

PROMPT 2 - Finding Privacy
Profile view of a woman in her early thirties with straight dark brown hair in a low ponytail, pale skin, athletic build, wearing a charcoal gray wool coat and black leather boots walking toward a concrete pillar on a modern campus. The camera tracks alongside her in a slow lateral movement as she moves from the open plaza into a shadowed alcove behind the pillar. The Zug wind whips at her collar and hair. Dramatic side lighting creates strong contrast between the bright plaza and the darker corner she's seeking. Vertical 9:16 composition emphasizing her solitary movement away from crowds of students in the soft-focus background. Cinematic shallow depth of field with muted, cold color grading.

PROMPT 3 - The Burner Phone Call

The camera is positioned directly behind her head and left shoulder, showing only the back of her hair and coat, with her hand visible holding the phone. In the soft-focus background, students walk past unaware. Cold daylight filters through the concrete campus architecture creating harsh shadows. Vertical 9:16 framing with the pillar's texture prominent in the foreground. Desaturated color palette emphasizing the clandestine nature of the conversation.

PROMPT 4 - Watching the Innocent
Side profile silhouette of a woman in her early thirties with straight dark brown hair in a low ponytail, pale skin, athletic build, wearing a charcoal gray wool coat, standing in shadow behind a concrete pillar. The camera captures her from the side as her head is turned, watching a group of laughing students in the bright plaza beyond. The contrast between her darkened figure in the foreground and the brightly lit, oblivious students in the background creates dramatic tension. Vertical 9:16 composition with strong backlighting creating a rim light effect on her profile. Shallow depth of field with the students becoming bokeh shapes of light and movement. Cold, clinical color grading.

Slow motion capture at the moment the phone moves away, suggesting the weight of the ended conversation.

PROMPT 6 - Checking the Wallet

Vertical 9:16 framing with the phone screen as the focal point. Shallow depth of field with everything except the hands and screen in soft focus. Clinical, cold color grading with the screen's glow creating an eerie luminescence.

PROMPT 7 - The Weight of Blood Money

PROMPT 8 - Walking Away with the Burden
