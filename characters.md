# The Lost Coins — Characters

## Protagonists

### Lena Vance — The Scout / Double Agent
- **Role**: High-end fixer and intermediary for Zheng. Flipped by the FBI in Ch32.
- **Appearance**: Long blonde hair in a loose ponytail, blue eyes, athletic build, mid-30s. Usually wears a charcoal blazer.
- **Arc**: Operates in Zheng's shadow world until the FBI gives her a choice — 20 years or cooperation. Chooses to be wired for the climax raid. Ends up pregnant (discovered Ch44).
- **Key trait**: Moves through the world with effortless, dangerous grace. Ice on the surface, conflict underneath.

### Paul Arndt — The Crypto Hacker
- **Role**: Quantum cryptography specialist. Runs Aether Quantum, a struggling startup in Zug, Switzerland.
- **Appearance**: Dark disheveled curly hair, light stubble, dark hoodie, lean build.
- **Arc**: Recruited by Lena to recover lost Bitcoin wallets. Joins the California operation believing it will prove he's the right man for Erica — that he can finally give her the financial stability she deserves. His loyalty to that hope is what makes him dangerous to control.
- **Key trait**: Technically brilliant, emotionally driven by the need to prove himself.

### Erica Voss — The Neuroscientist
- **Role**: Academic neuroscientist specialising in synaptic mapping. Paul's girlfriend.
- **Appearance**: Long dark hair, brown eyes, slender build, early 30s. Usually wears a white lab coat over dark clothing.
- **Arc**: Believes the California trip is a legitimate (if unorthodox) research engagement. The most "innocent" of the three — and therefore the one with the most to lose. Ends up pregnant (discovered Ch43).
- **Key trait**: Impeccable academic record, commanding in the lab, the emotional centre of the group.

## Antagonists

### Mr. Zheng — The Macau Crime Boss
- **Role**: Shadow financier. For twenty years the "plumbing for the world's filth." Orchestrates the Hal Finney wallet scheme as his exit from the underworld — the score that turns him from middleman to king.
- **Appearance**: Mid-50s, silver-streaked black hair slicked back, perfectly tailored dark charcoal suits.
- **Motivation**: Not just wealth — legitimacy. He wants to own the room, not just pass through it.
- **Key trait**: Cold, commanding, patient. Treats people as line items on a ledger.

### Mr. Gonzales ("Speedy") — The Cartel Operator
- **Role**: Mexican cartel logistics. Handles the physical operation: the Alcor infiltration, the body transport, the Sequoia warehouse setup.
- **Appearance**: Heavyset, broad-shouldered, salt-and-pepper mustache, dark canvas jacket, heavy work boots.
- **Key trait**: Professional criminal. Relaxed but focused — always on the clock.

## Law Enforcement

### Inspector Eddie Pinto — FBI / Interpol Liaison
- **Role**: The investigator who has been tracking Zheng's network. Flies from Hong Kong to Bern to coordinate with Swiss authorities.
- **Backstory**: His father was a foreman at the port in Macau — an honest man who lost everything to shadow financiers like Zheng. Pinto's entire career is a personal vendetta disguised as a vocation.
- **Key trait**: Exceptionally invested. This isn't just a case — it's an eviction notice.

### Captain Meier — Swiss Federal Police (Fedpol)
- **Role**: Swiss counterpart to Pinto. Provides the legal framework for letting the trio fly to California while the FBI sets the hook.
- **Key trait**: Methodical, cautious, reluctantly respectful of Pinto's instincts.
