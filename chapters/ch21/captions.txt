Lena stands on her balcony above Lake Zug
Encrypted line open to Macau

"Paperwork," Zheng says. The word sounds like an insult.
"She wants a trail for a ghost."

He has a team in Panama. Medical foundations, IRB seals, donor consent forms.
More official-looking than the real thing.

"She'll verify them," Lena warns.
"Let her try. The lawyers will have active bar numbers."

Then: "Use your charms, Lena. Make her feel chosen."
The memory of Erica's clarity flashes through her mind.

Lena says: I'll handle it.
She stays on the balcony a long time, watching the lake

She doesn't want to be the charm in Zheng's arsenal
But she is too deep to turn back
