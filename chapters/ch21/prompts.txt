# Chapter 21 - Runway Video Prompts (veo3 text-to-video)

**PROMPT 1 - Lena on the Balcony at Lake Zug**
Profile view of a 30-year-old woman, 1.70m tall, with long blonde hair blowing in a gentle breeze, blue eyes, wearing a silk robe over dark clothing, standing on a modern apartment balcony. Camera captures her from the side as she leans on the glass railing, looking down at the shimmering surface of Lake Zug below. Early morning mist rises from the water, with the distant Swiss mountains visible in soft blue haze. Cool blue-gray color grading with touches of warm gold from the rising sun catching her hair. Cinematic shallow depth of field, vertical 9:16 composition emphasizing her solitary figure against the vast landscape.

**PROMPT 2 - The Encrypted Phone Call**
Extreme close-up of a woman's hand, wearing a luxury Swiss watch, holding a matte black encrypted phone to her ear. Only the side of her jaw, her blonde hair falling past her shoulder, and the phone are visible. The phone's screen shows a scrambled caller ID with a Macau country code. Cold blue morning light from a window creates sharp highlights on the phone's surface. In the blurred background, the glass balcony railing and misty lake are barely distinguishable. Macro photography style with ultra-shallow depth of field, cinematic tension, vertical 9:16 framing focusing on the device and the covert nature of the communication.
