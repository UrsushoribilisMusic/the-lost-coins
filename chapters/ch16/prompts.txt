# Chapter 16 - Runway Video Prompts (veo3 text-to-video)

PROMPT 1 - Paul Alone in His Dark Office
Over-the-shoulder view from behind a man in his late 30s with dark brown disheveled hair, pale skin, wearing a wrinkled gray t-shirt, sitting hunched at a desk in a dark home office. His face is turned three-quarters away from camera as he stares at multiple trading monitors casting aggressive neon green and red light across the dim room. The monitors display Bitcoin charts with downward trending lines, their glow reflecting off the desk surface and illuminating papers scattered around. Cinematic shallow depth of field, high contrast lighting with deep shadows, moody atmosphere emphasizing isolation and stress. Camera slowly pushes in from behind his right shoulder in vertical 9:16 framing.

PROMPT 2 - Hand Rubbing Tired Eyes
Extreme close-up of a pale-skinned male hand with slightly trembling fingers rising up to rub exhausted eyes, shot from a side profile angle in a dark room. The hand moves slowly, wearily, with neon green and red light from off-screen monitors casting colored shadows across the knuckles and palm. Background is completely out of focus with bokeh effects from the monitor glow. Cinematic macro lens, shallow depth of field, slow motion at 60fps, emphasizing the physical toll of stress and exhaustion. Vertical 9:16 framing captures the gesture from fingertips to forearm.

PROMPT 3 - Bitcoin Price Crash on Monitors
Close-up of multiple computer monitors displaying Bitcoin trading charts with dramatic red downward candlesticks and percentage drops of -12% visible on screen, shot at a slight side angle. The monitors are arranged in a dark room with their aggressive red and green backlighting creating lens flares and reflections. Numbers and graphs show cascading price drops with volume indicators spiking. A silhouette of a person's head and shoulders is barely visible in the foreground, out of focus. Cinematic high-contrast lighting, sharp focus on the screen data, moody tech aesthetic with cyberpunk color grading. Vertical 9:16 framing emphasizing the vertical chart drops.

PROMPT 4 - Typing the Encrypted Email

Cinematic shallow depth of field focused on the hands, slow deliberate typing rhythm, tension visible in the finger movements. Vertical 9:16 framing captures hands from wrist to fingertips.

Vertical 9:16 framing focuses tightly on hand and trackpad.

PROMPT 7 - Black Sedan from Above
High-angle establishing shot looking down at a sleek black sedan with mirror-finish tinted windows idling at the end of a quiet European street at dusk. The car is positioned in three-quarter view, its surface reflecting surrounding streetlights and building facades in the glossy black paint and chrome accents. Warm apartment windows glow in the background buildings, one window showing faint green and red monitor light. Cinematic drone-style aerial perspective, moody blue hour lighting, shallow depth of field with the sedan in sharp focus and background softly blurred. Vertical 9:16 framing captures the car and street from above.

PROMPT 8 - Directional Microphone Aimed at Window
Close-up side profile of a long-range directional parabolic microphone mounted on a stand inside a dark car interior, aimed toward a distant apartment window. The microphone is professional surveillance equipment, metallic gray and black, with its dish catching faint reflections of streetlight. Through the car's windshield in the background, softly out of focus, an apartment building with one window glowing with green and red monitor light is visible.
