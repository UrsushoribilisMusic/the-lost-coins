Chapter 25 - The Shadow on the Vine

A luxury Escalade moves through California wine country, carrying three key figures toward a private estate in Napa.

Inside the Escalade, Paul relaxes, commenting that "The client really knows how to handle a delay." Erica observes Lena with growing fascination while questioning whether customs delays could genuinely affect someone wealthy enough to build a hidden laboratory. Lena deflects smoothly, attributing it to eccentric donor behavior. She receives an encrypted message: equipment delayed at Oakland's port, 48-hour window, maintain the group's contentment.

In a law enforcement surveillance operation, Inspector Pinto and FBI Special Agent Vance monitor a tracking signal showing the group heading northward toward Napa. They discuss strategy. Pinto identifies Lena as the psychological anchor holding the team stable. They decide to allow the targets to settle before moving, fearing premature exposure could trigger their employer to eliminate the principals.

As the vehicle enters a gated limestone villa, Lena experiences a momentary sensation of being observed, though the road behind appears empty. She welcomes her companions with professional warmth, holding Erica's gaze meaningfully as she says:

"Welcome to Napa."
