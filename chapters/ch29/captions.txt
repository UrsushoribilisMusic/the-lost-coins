Erica's sister sent an ultrasound this morning
A smudge of light that means someone new is coming

Paul covers her hand and says: we could do that
After we change the world. A dog. A house. You round and furious at me.

Erica laughs. A real laugh. No calculation.
Lena watches them and feels the weight of what she has built

She describes herself as a confluencer
Someone who moves people and money in the right direction

But she has been moving these two toward something
That does not care about their dreams at all

The candles burn low. The wine runs out.
And Lena carries a lie too large to put down
